Use an adjustment when the real account balance differs from Renalo because of an opening error, rounding correction, or activity that should not be represented as a transaction.

<ScreenshotPair desktop={desktop} mobile={mobile} alt="Account balance adjustments" />

Enter either:

- a signed adjustment amount, or
- the desired target balance, from which Renalo derives the required adjustment.

Positive adjustments increase the account balance; negative adjustments reduce it. They affect total balance but not income, expense, inflow, or outflow reports.

Current balance is calculated through today in UTC and excludes future-dated records. Deleting an adjustment removes its effect. Add a clear explanation so later reviewers understand why the correction exists.
