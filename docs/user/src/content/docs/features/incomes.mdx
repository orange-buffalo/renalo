Income records add money to an account. Income categories are separate from expense categories, so labels such as salary, royalties, teaching, or sales remain meaningful.

<ScreenshotPair desktop={desktop} mobile={mobile} alt="Income overview with posted and planned entries" />

## Organize the overview

The area chart follows the overview's date range, income-category, account, and notes filters. It automatically groups available values into daily, weekly, or monthly calendar buckets in your default account's currency. Foreign income uses its saved comparison value based on actual transfers; income without conversion evidence is omitted from the chart. The chart starts visible on desktop and hidden on mobile; use the chart icon beside the grouping control to change its visibility.

Use the grouping control beside **More filters** to show a plain list, group income by date, or group it by income category. Group headers show a separate total for every currency on the right. The selected income grouping is remembered on this device and does not change the grouping used for expenses.

## Add income

Select **Add income**, then choose its date, income category, account, and amount. Notes are optional. The amount is interpreted using the selected account's currency.

<ScreenshotPair desktop={formDesktop} mobile={formMobile} alt="Create income form" />

Future income remains planned until its date. Planned totals stay grouped by currency; Renalo never adds unrelated currencies into one misleading number. On mobile, tap the **Planned incomes** card to show its planned entries immediately.
