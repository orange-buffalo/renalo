<ScreenshotPair desktop={desktop} mobile={mobile} alt="Renalo dashboard with account balance cards" />

## What the numbers mean

**Total balance** starts with the account's opening balance, adds posted income and incoming transfers, subtracts posted expenses and outgoing transfers, and includes manual balance adjustments. Renalo uses today's date in your browser's timezone, so activity after your local today is excluded.

**Inflow** includes this month's posted income and incoming transfer amount for that account. **Outflow** includes posted expenses and outgoing transfers. The current month also follows your browser's timezone. Transfers use the amount belonging to each side's account currency.

Archived accounts are absent from the dashboard but keep their records.

## Record something quickly

Use **Record new** to open an expense, income, or transfer form without first visiting its overview.
