The dashboard gives every active account its own card, currency, current balance, and month-to-date money flow.

<ScreenshotPair desktop={desktop} mobile={mobile} alt="Renalo dashboard with account balance cards" />

## What the numbers mean

**Total balance** starts with the account's opening balance, adds posted income and incoming transfers, subtracts posted expenses and outgoing transfers, and includes manual balance adjustments. Activity after today is excluded.

**Inflow** includes this month's posted income and incoming transfer amount for that account. **Outflow** includes posted expenses and outgoing transfers. Transfers use the amount belonging to each side's account currency.

Archived accounts are absent from the dashboard but keep their records.

## Record something quickly

Use **Record new** to open an expense, income, or transfer form without first visiting its overview.
