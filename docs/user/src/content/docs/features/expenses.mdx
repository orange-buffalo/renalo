Each expense belongs to one account and one expense category. Amounts are positive values; the expense type determines that they reduce the account balance.

<ScreenshotPair desktop={desktop} mobile={mobile} alt="Expense overview with posted and planned entries" />

## Record an expense

1. Select **Add expense**.
2. Choose the date, category, and account.
3. Enter the amount in the selected account's currency.
4. Add optional notes.
5. Enable recurrence when this expense follows a schedule.

Dates are date-only values. Today's and past entries affect current balances; future entries appear as planned activity.

## Find the right entries

The global date range is shared across transaction and transfer overviews. **More filters** narrows expenses by account, category, or notes, while archived accounts and categories are excluded from selectors.

Use the grouping control beside **More filters** to switch between a plain list, groups by date, or groups by expense category. Date and category headers show the combined amount for each currency on the right, without mixing currencies into one total. Renalo remembers the selected expense grouping on this device independently from the income overview.

<ScreenshotPair desktop={filtersDesktop} mobile={filtersMobile} alt="Expense filtering controls" />

On mobile, tap a compact row card to reveal secondary details and edit/delete actions; the date remains visible while collapsed.
